Detects Smoke & Harmful Gases
About Project
The motive of this project is to help an individual to monitor air quality around them. So, we have designed an IOT which can detect harmful gasses around its surroundings. For our IOT, we have used Raspberry Pi.
App Features
Our app not only shows you readings but also notifies you when the AQI reaches a critical level, so that you can take preventive measures. App also suggests you methods on how you can improve air around you.
